Terms of use for Sync2Time

1. Use of Sync2Time

Sync2Time is provided for authorized Sync2VA Online Learning Corp. administrators and employees to track attendance, review pay information, manage requests, generate paystubs, and synchronize approved pay information with accounting systems such as QuickBooks Online.

2. Authorized users only

Users must use only their assigned account and must not share passwords or attempt to access another employee’s records. Administrators are responsible for granting, updating, and removing employee access.

3. Accurate records

Users agree to enter accurate time, task, leave, overtime, and request information. Any payroll edits, adjustments, or approvals should follow company policy and must be supported by notes where required.

4. QuickBooks Online integration

When connected by an authorized admin, Sync2Time may send approved employee pay information to QuickBooks Online to create accounting journal entries. Sync2Time does not run QuickBooks Payroll. Users must ensure pay information has been reviewed and approved before syncing.

5. Confidentiality

Payroll, attendance, paystub, and employee information is confidential. Users must not disclose, download, or distribute confidential records except as required for authorized business purposes.

6. Availability and changes

Sync2Time may be updated, changed, interrupted, or discontinued as needed for business operations, compliance, or security. Continued use of Sync2Time means users accept the current terms.

7. Contact

For questions about these terms, contact Sync2VA HR at hr@sync2va.com.
