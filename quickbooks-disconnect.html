Disconnect QuickBooks from Sync2Time

Sync2VA Online Learning Corp. · QuickBooks Online integration

How to disconnect

If you want to disconnect QuickBooks Online from Sync2Time, contact Sync2VA HR or the Sync2Time administrator. The administrator will remove the QuickBooks connection from Sync2Time and revoke the stored QuickBooks OAuth tokens.

Email HR to disconnect

After disconnecting

  • Sync2Time will stop sending approved employee pay information to QuickBooks Online accounting journal entries.
  • Previously synced QuickBooks journal entries will remain in QuickBooks unless your QuickBooks admin edits or deletes them directly.
  • Sync2Time attendance and payroll records will remain available inside Sync2Time according to company retention rules.

Reconnect

To reconnect later, sign in to Sync2Time as an admin, open the Payroll page, and choose Connect QuickBooks.

Open Sync2Time Payroll
