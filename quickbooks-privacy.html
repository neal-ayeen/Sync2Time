Sync2Time Privacy Policy

Sync2VA Online Learning Corp. · Effective July 20, 2026

QuickBooks Online integration

Overview

Sync2Time is an internal time tracking and payroll support application used by Sync2VA Online Learning Corp. This Privacy Policy explains what information Sync2Time collects, how it is used, and how the QuickBooks Online integration works.

Information we collect

  • Employee profile details such as name, work email, role, department, schedule, pay rate, and paystub delivery email.
  • Time tracking records such as clock-in time, clock-out time, task/category, work notes, lunch status, and attendance changes.
  • Payroll calculation data such as approved hours, overtime, adjustments, deductions, commissions, exchange rate used, gross pay, and net pay.
  • Request records such as leave requests, overtime requests, time edit requests, approvals, and audit logs.
  • QuickBooks connection information stored securely in backend systems, including OAuth tokens required to create accounting records.

How we use information

We use this information to operate attendance tracking, payroll review, paystub preparation, employee request approvals, and accounting sync. When QuickBooks Online is connected, Sync2Time sends approved employee pay information to QuickBooks to create accounting journal entries for pay expense and pay payable/clearing records. Sync2Time does not run QuickBooks Payroll.

QuickBooks data access

Sync2Time requests access to QuickBooks Online Accounting data only for business accounting synchronization. Sync2Time does not sell QuickBooks data. Sync2Time does not use QuickBooks data for advertising. Access tokens are stored in the backend and are not exposed in public website files or browser code.

Sharing and retention

Information is shared only with systems needed to operate Sync2Time, including Supabase for application data storage, QuickBooks Online for accounting sync when enabled, and approved email/payroll delivery services where applicable. Records are retained as required for internal payroll, audit, accounting, and legal purposes.

Security

Sync2Time uses authenticated access controls and backend functions for privileged operations. QuickBooks secrets and OAuth tokens must be stored only in Supabase Edge Function secrets or backend database tables protected from direct browser access.

Contact

For privacy questions or access requests, contact Sync2VA HR at hr@sync2va.com.
